MINISTRY OF AYUSH

The Ministry of AYUSH (Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy) is a government body in India dedicated to promoting and regulating traditional Indian systems of medicine.

 

It oversees the development, education, and research in these alternative therapies, aiming to integrate them into mainstream healthcare and ensure their quality and efficacy.
